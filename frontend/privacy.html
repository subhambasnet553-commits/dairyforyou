Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

What this is

Dairy for You is a personal project built for couples to keep a shared, time-delayed diary. It isn't run by a company — just treat it accordingly: as a small, honest personal app rather than a commercial product with a legal team behind it.

What we collect

When you sign up, we store your first name, last name, email address, and a securely hashed version of your password (we never store or see your actual password). If you choose to add a profile picture and bio, those are stored too. Diary entries you write are stored with the date they were written.

How your diary entries work

Your entries are visible to you immediately. They become visible to your paired partner only after your chosen unlock period has passed (30 days by default, adjustable in your preferences). Nobody else — not even the app's creator — reads your entries as part of normal operation.

Where it's stored

Data is stored using MongoDB Atlas, a third-party database provider. Standard security practices are used (password hashing, authenticated API access), but as with any small independent project, no system can guarantee absolute security.

What we don't do

We don't sell your data. We don't share it with advertisers. We don't use it to train anything. There are no ads in this app.

Your control over your data

You can update or delete your profile information at any time from your profile panel. You can unpair from your partner, or delete your account entirely, both from the Security tab.

Questions

Since this is a personal project, reach out directly to whoever shared this app with you if you have questions or concerns about your data.
